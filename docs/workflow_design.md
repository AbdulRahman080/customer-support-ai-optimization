## Channel-Based AI Automation Strategy

Analysis results showed that workflow efficiency varies significantly across support channels.

Based on these findings, a hybrid AI-assisted workflow is proposed.

### Phone Channel

Customers are offered a choice between an AI voice assistant and a human agent.

The AI voice assistant handles:
- billing questions
- refund status
- subscription cancellation
- product compatibility questions

Complex cases automatically escalate to human agents.

This reduces queue wait times while preserving accessibility for customers who prefer human support.

---

### Chat Channel

Chat interactions already show the highest customer satisfaction.

Instead of replacing agents, AI is used to assist them through:
- automatic ticket summaries
- suggested responses
- routing hints

This preserves quality while improving agent efficiency.

---

### Email and Social Media Channels

Email and social media tickets show the highest friction scores and longest resolution times.

AI-assisted triage is introduced at intake:

1. generate ticket summary
2. classify ticket type
3. predict urgency
4. recommend routing queue

High-confidence predictions can be auto-routed while low-confidence predictions are reviewed by agents.

---

### Refund and Cancellation Requests

Refund and cancellation requests are strong candidates for automation due to their rule-based structure.

The workflow includes:

- eligibility verification
- policy validation
- automated approval when applicable
- escalation when exceptions occur

This reduces resolution time significantly.

---

### Technical Issue Support

Technical issues are supported through AI troubleshooting assistance.

The workflow includes:

- identifying issue type
- suggesting resolution steps
- escalating unresolved cases to specialists

This reduces support workload while improving response speed.

---

### Escalation Prediction Layer

Priority labels alone did not strongly predict faster resolution times.

A predictive escalation layer is proposed using:

- resolution delay indicators
- ticket type
- response delay signals
- customer effort score

This enables earlier intervention for high-risk tickets.
